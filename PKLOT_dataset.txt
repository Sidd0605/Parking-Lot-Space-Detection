PKLOT Dataset - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/blanderbuss/parking-lot-dataset

The PKLot dataset is a collection of images of parking lots extracted from surveillance camera frames. The dataset contains 12,416 images of parking lots and 695,899 images of parking spaces segmented from them, which were manually checked and labeled. The parking spaces are labeled as occupied or empty, and the images were captured from different parking lots under varied weather conditions, including sunny, cloudy, and rainy days. The dataset is available for research purposes under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 License. The PKLot dataset is a robust dataset for parking lot classification and can be used for object detection tasks.
